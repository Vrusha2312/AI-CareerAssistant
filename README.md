## Summary
AI Career Assistant is an AI-powered platform that helps students and job seekers identify suitable career paths based on their skills, education, and interests. It uses artificial intelligence techniques like Natural Language Processing and recommendation systems to suggest career options, learning resources, and job opportunities.
Building AI course project
AI Career Assistant is an artificial intelligence-based platform designed to help students and job seekers make better career decisions. Many people struggle to identify suitable career paths because they do not have access to personalized guidance about skills, courses, and job opportunities. This project aims to provide an intelligent assistant that analyzes a user's education, skills, interests, and career goals to recommend suitable career options.
The system uses AI techniques such as Natural Language Processing (NLP), machine learning, and recommendation systems to understand user information, analyze skill requirements, and provide personalized suggestions. It can help users identify missing skills, discover relevant learning resources, improve their resumes, and find suitable internship or job opportunities.
The main goal of this project is to make career guidance more accessible, especially for students and fresh graduates who may not have professional career counseling support. By combining AI with educational and employment information, the platform can assist users in planning their career journey more effectively.
This project demonstrates the practical application of artificial intelligence to solve a real-world problem and shows how AI can support people in making informed career decisions.
